6.57.2 ACLE Intrinsics for AArch64

6.57.2.1 CRC32 intrinsics

These intrinsics are available when the CRC32 architecture extension is specified, e.g. when the -march=armv8-a+crc switch is used, or when the target processor specified with -mcpu supports it.

  • uint32_t __crc32b (uint32_t, uint8_t)
    Form of expected instruction(s): crc32b w0, w1, w2
  • uint32_t __crc32h (uint32_t, uint16_t)
    Form of expected instruction(s): crc32h w0, w1, w2
  • uint32_t __crc32w (uint32_t, uint32_t)
    Form of expected instruction(s): crc32w w0, w1, w2
  • uint32_t __crc32d (uint32_t, uint64_t)
    Form of expected instruction(s): crc32x w0, w1, x2
  • uint32_t __crc32cb (uint32_t, uint8_t)
    Form of expected instruction(s): crc32cb w0, w1, w2
  • uint32_t __crc32ch (uint32_t, uint16_t)
    Form of expected instruction(s): crc32ch w0, w1, w2
  • uint32_t __crc32cw (uint32_t, uint32_t)
    Form of expected instruction(s): crc32cw w0, w1, w2
  • uint32_t __crc32cd (uint32_t, uint64_t)
    Form of expected instruction(s): crc32cx w0, w1, x2
